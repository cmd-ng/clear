ABOUT

We invest in a better, sustainable future.

Clearsite Holdings is a black-owned and controlled company formed on strong entrepreneurial principles investing in innovative solutions that positively impact on sustainable social, environmental, and economic development across Africa.

Growth focused investment

At Clearsite Holdings, we are more than an investment company; we are at the forefront of integrating sustainability with financial growth with a clear vision for the future of Africa's potential.

Proudly Level 1 B-BBEE

Clearsite Holdings is 100% black owned entity founded by black industrialists who are passionate about the development of rail and energy infrastructure projects on the continent.

Led by experienced industrialists

The business is led by highly experienced, well networked Black industrialists, all of whom have created consistent, successful organic growth and returns for private and listed organisations.
